Billing & Payments

Understand how pricing works and manage your payment methods.

Free to deploy

Every stack on Deploy Box is free to deploy — no setup fees or per-container charges. You only pay for the plan you choose as your usage grows.

Visit the pricing page for a full breakdown of plans and what's included.

Payment methods

Deploy Box uses Stripe for secure payment processing. You can add and manage credit or debit cards from your organization's billing page.

  • Navigate to your Organization Dashboard → Billing
  • Click Add Payment Method to add a card
  • Your card details are securely handled by Stripe — Deploy Box never stores them directly

Billing cycle

Billing is handled at the organization level on a monthly cycle. At the end of each billing period, usage is calculated and charged to the payment method on file.

Usage tracking

Deploy Box automatically tracks resource usage across all stacks in your organization. You can view current usage from the billing dashboard at any time.
