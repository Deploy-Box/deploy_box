Organizations

The top-level container for your team, projects, and billing.

What is an organization?

An organization is the highest-level grouping in Deploy Box. It owns projects, manages team members, and handles billing. Think of it like a company or team workspace.

Creating an organization

From your dashboard, click Create Organization and give it a name. You'll be set as the owner automatically.

Tip: You can create multiple organizations to keep personal work separate from client or team projects.

Team members

Invite team members to collaborate on projects within your organization. From the Members tab in your organization dashboard you can:

  • Invite existing Deploy Box users by email
  • Invite new users who don't have an account yet
  • Manage member roles and permissions
  • Remove members from the organization

Organization settings

The Settings page lets you update your organization name and manage general configuration. The Billing page shows your current plan, usage, and payment methods.
