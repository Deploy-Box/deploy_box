Current Stacks

Stacks are our way of delivering easy to use containerized tech-stacks that are ready out of the box. We support all popular stacks, including some of our own. We also have a diverse community of stack developers for entreprenuers looking for full stack support to help bring thier ideas to life. Check out some of our current stacks below.

Most Popular Stack

MERN

MERN stands for MongoDB, Express.js, React, and Node.js. It is a modern stack that uses a combination of modern frameworks and a no sql database to ensure you spin up your webapp as quickly as possible.

For Angular Fans

MEAN

MEAN stands for MongoDB, Express.js, Angular, and Node.js. It is one of the most used tech stacks but requires a slightly higher learning curve than our MERN stack.

Full Stack Python

DJANGO

DJANGO is a high-level Python web framework that encourages rapid development and clean, pragmatic design. It’s perfect for building robust web applications quickly.

Classic Web Stack

LAMP

LAMP stands for Linux, Apache, MySQL, and PHP. It’s a proven open-source stack for building dynamic websites and web apps, trusted by millions of developers for its stability and flexibility.

Vue.js Stack

MEVN

MEVN stands for MongoDB, Express.js, Vue.js, and Node.js. It’s a modern JavaScript stack that leverages Vue for a progressive frontend experience and Node/Express for a robust backend

Our Team

We are a dynamic team of recent college graduates passionate about simplifying the development process and bridging the gap between skilled developers and exciting career opportunities. Our journey began with a shared vision: to create a platform that empowers developers and accelerates how companies build, deploy, and scale their applications.

Our company provides pre-containerized versions of the most popular tech stacks, enabling developers to bypass the time-consuming setup process and focus on what really matters—writing code and building innovative solutions. By offering these ready-to-go development environments, we remove the friction of configuring software, dependencies, and infrastructure, allowing developers to spin up environments in minutes.

In addition to providing powerful tools, we also operate a marketplace designed to connect full-stack developers with companies seeking top-tier talent. Whether you’re a developer seeking your next freelance project or a company looking for talent, our platform helps you connect with the right opportunities.

Our mission is to foster a community where developers can grow, collaborate, and thrive while delivering high-quality solutions to businesses. We're dedicated to making development more accessible, streamlined, and efficient—helping everyone reach their fullest potential.
