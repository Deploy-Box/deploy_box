Current Stacks

Stacks are our way of delivering easy to use containerized tech-stacks that are ready out of the box. We support all popular stacks, including some of our own. We also have a diverse community of stack developers for entreprenuers looking for full stack support to help bring thier ideas to life. Check out some of our current stacks below.

Most Popuar Stack

MERN

MERN stands for MondoDB, Express.js, React, and Node.js. It is a modern stack that uses a combination of modern frameworks and a no sql database to ensure you spin up your webapp as quicky as possible.

For Angular Fans

MEAN

MEAN stands for MondoDB, Express.js, Angular, and Node.js. It is one of the most used tech stacks but requires a slightly higher learning curve than our MERN stack.

Our Team

We are a dynamic team of recent college graduates who are passionate about simplifying the development process and bridging the gap between skilled developers and exciting career opportunities. Our journey began with a shared vision: to create a platform that not only empowers developers but also accelerates the way companies build, deploy, and scale their applications.

Our company provides pre-containerized versions of the most popular tech stacks, enabling developers to bypass the time-consuming setup process and focus on what really matters—writing code and building innovative solutions. By offering these ready-to-go development environments, we remove the friction of configuring software, dependencies, and infrastructure, allowing developers to spin up environments in a matter of minutes instead of hours or days.

In addition to providing powerful tools for developers, we also operate a marketplace designed to connect full-stack developers with companies looking for top-tier talent. Whether you’re a developer seeking your next freelance project, a remote position, or a full-time opportunity, our platform helps you connect with businesses that align with your skills and career goals. Companies, on the other hand, benefit from a curated pool of talented developers ready to contribute to their projects, saving time and effort in the hiring process.

Our mission is to foster a community where developers can grow, collaborate, and thrive while delivering high-quality solutions to businesses in need of innovation. We're dedicated to making development more accessible, streamlined, and efficient—helping developers and companies alike reach their fullest potential. As recent graduates ourselves, we understand the challenges of navigating the tech world and are committed to empowering others through better tools, meaningful opportunities, and an ecosystem built for success.
