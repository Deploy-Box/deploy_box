Frequently Asked Questions

What does free to deploy mean?

All stacks have zero setup fees. You only pay your monthly plan for hosting and resources.

Can I upgrade or downgrade?

Yes! Change plans anytime from your dashboard. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.

What happens if I exceed my plan limits?

Overage charges apply automatically at transparent rates. You'll never lose access to your stacks.

Do I own the code?

Absolutely. The stack is deployed for you to use, but the code is yours to modify, export, and deploy elsewhere if you choose.
