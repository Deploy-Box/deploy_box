## Getting Started

1. **Visit [Deploy Box](https://deploy-box.onrender.com)** and create your account
2. **Create an Organization** to manage your projects and team members
3. **Create a Project** within your organization
4. **Purchase a Stack** that fits your application needs
5. **Deploy and Configure** using our intuitive web dashboard

That's it! Your stack will be deployed and ready to use.
